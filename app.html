No-Cook Weight Loss Tips
💧
Hydrate first
Drink 16 oz water before every meal — reduces hunger and costs zero calories.
🥚
Prioritize protein
Protein keeps you full longer. Aim for at least 0.7g per lb of your goal body weight daily.
⏰
Eat in a window
Try to eat within a 10-hour window (e.g. 8am–6pm) to naturally reduce intake.
🚶
Walk after meals
A 10-min walk after eating reduces blood sugar spikes and burns 50–100 extra calories.
